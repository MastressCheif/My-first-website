2. Kruger National Park

Africa's Premier Wildlife Destination


Kruger National Park is one of Africa's largest and most renowned game reserves, spanning nearly 20,000 square kilometers. It is home to an incredible diversity of wildlife, including the Big Five: lion, leopard, rhinoceros, elephant, and buffalo. The park offers a range of safari experiences, from guided tours to self-drive adventures.

About Kruger National Park:

Kruger National Park is one of Africa's largest and most renowned game reserves, spanning nearly 20,000 square kilometers. It is home to an incredible diversity of wildlife, including the Big Five: lion, leopard, rhinoceros, elephant, and buffalo. The park offers a range of safari experiences, from guided tours to self-drive adventures.

Highlights:

  • One of Africa's largest game reserves
  • Big Five sightings: lion, leopard, rhino, elephant, buffalo
  • Guided safaris and self-drives
  • Luxury lodges and rustic camps

Why go:

Incredible wildlife encounters and a must-visit for nature lovers.
