3. The Garden Route

Scenic Coastal Paradise


The Garden Route is a breathtaking stretch of coastline in South Africa, renowned for its stunning natural beauty and diverse ecosystems. This scenic drive runs from Mossel Bay to Storms River, offering travelers a unique blend of beaches, forests, mountains, and charming towns.

About the Garden Route:

The Garden Route is a stunning stretch of coastline in South Africa, known for its breathtaking scenery, diverse ecosystems, and charming towns. It runs from Mossel Bay to Storms River, offering a mix of beaches, forests, mountains, and lagoons.

Highlights:

  • Scenic coastal drive from Mossel Bay to Storms River
  • Tsitsikamma National Park, Krysna forests, Plettenberg Bay
  • Adventure sports: ziplining, kayaking, bungee jumping

Why go:

Perfect mix of ocean views, forests, mountains, and small-town charm.
